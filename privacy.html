Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 4, 2026

Local processing

PostBuddy processes your CSV and HTML templates locally in your browser. The extension does not upload these files or their contents to any external server.

No data collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit personal data. All email content and recipient information remains on your device.

Google OAuth

The extension uses Google OAuth to securely access your Gmail account for sending emails. OAuth tokens are handled by Google and used only for the sending action you initiate.

User consent

By using the extension, you consent to this policy and to the local processing described above.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, you can reach us at support@gmailbulksender.com.
